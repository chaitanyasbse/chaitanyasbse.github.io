OUR UNIQUE BRIQUETTE DESIGN (MORE EFFICIENT)

1

HONEY COMB LIKE STRUCTURE

This optimal structure can be easily realised by changes in moulds and can improve the heating by proving more air at surface and increasing the surface area.

2

CHARCOAL BRIQUETTES:

The Briquettes are taken through a process of carbonization . ADVANTAGES : Now these have a low ignition temperature or kindling point, produces less smoke,& has a higher calorific value as compared to before.

3

Efficiency

Uniform physical dimensions & combustion characteristics, results in more efficient energy conversion. Briquettes burn in a controlled manner, slow and efficiently because of lower moisture content, higher bulk density, & lower ash content.
